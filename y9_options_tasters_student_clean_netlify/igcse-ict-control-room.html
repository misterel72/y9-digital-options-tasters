Source Pack: FutureFest Control Room

Incoming message from Team Lead

Subject: Systems failing before FutureFest launch

The event goes live this afternoon. We have five problems across the digital materials: files are hard to find, the spreadsheet totals are wrong, the guest database is messy, the poster is confusing and the website has weak links.

Your team must inspect the evidence, make accurate fixes and decide which problems are most urgent. Do not guess. Use the source material below.

Mission rules

  • Use the evidence, not guesswork.
  • Hints cost points.
  • Accuracy matters more than speed.
  • Every final priority must be justified.
  • You may work in pairs or threes.

Scoring

+10 correct fix+20 strong final priority-5 hint usedBonus for clear explanation

A perfect team acts like a calm IT support desk, not like someone randomly unplugging things and hoping for the best.
